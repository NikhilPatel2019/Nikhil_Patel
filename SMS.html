SOCIETY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A web-based Housing Society Management solution for residential and commercial complexes & Housing Societies.An online society and accounting management portal. A unique platform which can be used by Cooperative Housing Societies / Resident Welfare Association (RWA) / Apartment Association etc. This platform expands transparency and aims to strengthen the bond among members and Managing Committee. In a matter of clicks, one can have access to maintenance bills, payments, agenda’s, meeting of minutes, circulars, notices and many more interesting features. It also provides an opportunity to list your business, connect to vendors and keeps you well-informed with all the events happening. It is one of the best integrated solutions for Housing Society accounting and billing. It’s an automated accounting & billing portal which promotes digitization of complete books of account.
